Education

Humans are made to learn, however many country's ways of teaching are far outdated. I plan to have Vita Novian students learn in a way that's made for today, not yesterday.

Today's world

Learning today involves learning how to use modern technology, and modern theories. During my time in office, I will work tirelessly to show to the people that there is a modern way to learn. One that doesn't require a child to sit down for seven hours a day. A way that let's children learn a mix of what they want, and teach them what they need to know.

Despite not having physical buildings, that doesn't mean that our citizens are not learning every single day. Many will begin learning our second national language, and I will make it my goal to insure that it's taught to those who want to learn it properly, and in a manner that best helps them.
