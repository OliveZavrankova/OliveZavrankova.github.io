Economics of the nation

A nation is unable to function and interact with other nations without a well developed economic system.

I plan to work closely with the department of National Economics to drive the economy forward and to help prevent it from crashing.

Why care?

To many, the economic state of their country is not on their mind every day, but it is on mine. When you lack a strong economy holding up your nation, everything else begins to fall. That is exactly why I plan to uphold my promise to create a strong economy from my first day, to my last. When it comes to being Prime Minister, there are no bad days.
