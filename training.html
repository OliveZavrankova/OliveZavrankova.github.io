Training the founders

The main job of the 13 Founders is the assist the Prime Minister and Vice Minister. 

In order for them to be able to do this however, they must be properly taught how to do just that. I plan to begin training all founders in a 2 week course, beginning within my first 30 days in office.

The 13 Founders will be trained in:

* Helping citizens with their needs
* Settling the House of Lawmakers
* Assisting the Prime Minister and Vice Minister
* Enforcing laws when needed to
